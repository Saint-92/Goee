routes

The Grand Voyager:
From Fjords to the Arctic

This epic route will take you from the heart of the Norwegian fjords to the harsh beauty of the Arctic Circle, revealing all of Norway's contrasts. Perfect for those who want to see the most impressive sights in one trip.

  • 14-16 days
  • ~2200 - 2500 km
Day 1:

Arrival in Bergen & Motorhome Pick-up

  • Activity: Arrive in Bergen, pick up your motorhome. Complete a thorough briefing and vehicle check. Stock up on groceries and supplies for the first 3-4 days.

  • Highlight: Evening stroll through the historic Bryggen Wharf, a UNESCO World Heritage site.

  • Overnight: Bergen Camping Park or similar certified motorhome parking.

Day 2:

Bergen to Flåm

  • Activity: Drive from Bergen to Flåm via the scenic roads along the fjords.

  • Highlight: Flåm Railway journey, one of the world's most beautiful train rides.

  • Overnight: Flåm Camping with stunning fjord views.

Day 3:

Flåm to Geiranger

  • Activity: Drive through stunning landscapes to Geiranger, home to one of Norway's most famous fjords.

  • Highlight: Geirangerfjord cruise to see the Seven Sisters and Bridal Veil waterfalls.

  • Overnight: Geiranger Camping.

Day 4:

Geiranger to Trollstigen

  • Activity: Drive the famous Troll's Path (Trollstigen) with its 11 hairpin turns.

  • Highlight: Viewing platforms overlooking the dramatic valley.

  • Overnight: Trollstigen Camping.

Day 5:

Atlantic Ocean Road

  • Activity: Drive the iconic Atlantic Ocean Road, connecting small islands with dramatic bridges.

  • Highlight: Photography stops along the winding road with ocean views.

  • Overnight: Camping along the Atlantic coast.

Alpine Adventure:
Austrian Mountain Odyssey

Discover the majestic Austrian Alps on this breathtaking route through snow-capped peaks, crystal-clear lakes, and charming mountain villages. Experience the best of Austrian culture and natural beauty.

  • 10-12 days
  • ~1200 - 1500 km
‹
›
Day 1:

Arrival in Vienna

  • Activity: Arrive in Vienna, pick up your vehicle. Explore the historic city center.

  • Highlight: Visit Schönbrunn Palace and St. Stephen's Cathedral.

  • Overnight: Vienna Camping or city hotel.

Day 2:

Vienna to Salzburg

  • Activity: Drive from Vienna to Salzburg through the Austrian countryside.

  • Highlight: Explore Mozart's birthplace and the Hohensalzburg Fortress.

  • Overnight: Salzburg Camping.

Day 3:

Salzburg to Hallstatt

  • Activity: Drive to the picturesque village of Hallstatt, a UNESCO World Heritage site.

  • Highlight: Boat tour on Hallstätter See and visit to the Salt Mine.

  • Overnight: Hallstatt Camping with lake views.

Day 4:

Grossglockner High Alpine Road

  • Activity: Drive the spectacular Grossglockner High Alpine Road.

  • Highlight: Panoramic views of Austria's highest mountain, Grossglockner.

  • Overnight: Alpine hut or camping in Heiligenblut.

Day 5:

Innsbruck and Tyrolean Alps

  • Activity: Drive to Innsbruck, capital of the Tyrol region.

  • Highlight: Visit the Golden Roof and take the cable car to Nordkette.

  • Overnight: Innsbruck Camping.
