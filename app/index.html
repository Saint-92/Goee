The Grand Voyager:
From Fjords to the Arctic

This epic route will take you from the heart of the Norwegian fjords to the harsh beauty of the Arctic Circle, revealing all of Norway's contrasts. Perfect for those who want to see the most impressive sights in one trip.

  • 14-16 days
  • ~2200 - 2500 km
Day 1-2:

Arrival in Bergen & City Exploration

  • Activity: Arrive Bergen Airport, motorhome pick-up and briefing. Day 1: Settle in, grocery shopping. Day 2: Full day exploring Bergen - Bryggen Wharf (UNESCO), Fish Market, Fløibanen funicular to Mount Fløyen.

  • Highlight: Panoramic views from Fløyen (320m), historic Hanseatic buildings at Bryggen, fresh seafood at the Fish Market.

  • Overnight: Bergen Camping Park (2 nights). Distance covered: Local exploration only.

Day 3:

Bergen to Flåm via Voss

  • Activity: Scenic drive E16 to Flåm (170km, 3 hours). Stop in Voss for lunch - adventure capital of Norway. Afternoon: Flåm Railway (Flåmsbana) journey.

  • Highlight: Flåm Railway - world's steepest railway gradient, waterfalls Kjosfossen & Rjoandefossen. Evening fjord walk in Aurlandsfjord.

  • Overnight: Flåm Camping. Driving distance: 170km.

Day 4:

Flåm to Geiranger

  • Activity: Drive via Sognefjellet Mountain Road (National Tourist Route). Optional morning fjord cruise on Naerøyfjord (UNESCO). Afternoon drive to Geiranger (200km, 4-5 hours).

  • Highlight: Geirangerfjord cruise (1.5 hours) past Seven Sisters, Bridal Veil waterfalls. Dalsnibba viewpoint (1500m) for stunning fjord panorama.

  • Overnight: Geiranger Camping. Driving distance: 200km.

Day 5:

Geiranger to Trollstigen & Åndalsnes

  • Activity: Drive Eagle Road (Ørnevegen) with 11 hairpin turns. Continue to Trollstigen (Troll's Path) - famous mountain road with viewing platforms. Evening in Åndalsnes.

  • Highlight: Trollstigen viewing platform overlooking Isterdalen valley with Stigfossen waterfall (320m drop). Romsdalseggen ridge views.

  • Overnight: Trollstigen Camping or Åndalsnes camping. Driving distance: 110km.

Day 6:

Atlantic Ocean Road to Kristiansund

  • Activity: Drive to Atlantic Ocean Road (Atlanterhavsveien) - 8.3km engineering marvel. Stop at each of 8 bridges for photography. Continue to Kristiansund.

  • Highlight: Storseisundet Bridge - iconic arch appearing to dive into ocean. Håholmen fishing village visit. Atlantic Road viewing platform.

  • Overnight: Kristiansund Camping. Driving distance: 250km.

Day 7:

Kristiansund to Trondheim

  • Activity: Coastal drive to Trondheim (190km, 3.5 hours). Afternoon exploring Norway's third largest city and former Viking capital.

  • Highlight: Nidaros Cathedral - Norway's national sanctuary. Bakklandet district with colorful wooden houses. Old Town Bridge (Gamle Bybro).

  • Overnight: Trondheim Camping. Driving distance: 190km.

Day 8:

Trondheim to Mo i Rana (Arctic Circle)

  • Activity: Drive north on E6 (420km, 6-7 hours). Cross the Arctic Circle at the Arctic Circle Center. Optional stop at Svartisen Glacier viewpoint.

  • Highlight: Arctic Circle crossing ceremony (certificate available). Svartisen Glacier - mainland Europe's second largest. Arrival in Mo i Rana - gateway to Arctic.

  • Overnight: Mo i Rana Camping. Driving distance: 420km.

Day 9:

Mo i Rana to Bodø

  • Activity: Drive to Bodø (170km, 3 hours). Visit Saltstraumen - world's strongest tidal maelstrom. Explore Bodø, European Capital of Culture 2024.

  • Highlight: Saltstraumen maelstrom viewing (check tide times). Norwegian Aviation Museum. Midnight sun viewing from Keiservarden viewpoint.

  • Overnight: Bodø Camping. Driving distance: 170km.

Day 10-11:

Lofoten Islands Exploration

  • Activity: Day 10: Ferry Bodø-Moskenes (3.5-4 hours). Drive to Reine. Day 11: Explore Lofoten - Å fishing village, Nusfjord (UNESCO candidate), beaches, optional hiking.

  • Highlight: Reinebringen hike (stairs) for iconic Lofoten view. Hamnøy and Sakrisøy fishing villages. Lofotr Viking Museum at Borg.

  • Overnight: Reine Camping or Lofoten Beach Camp (2 nights). Driving distance: 100km exploring.

Day 12-13:

Lofoten to Narvik & Northern Fjords

  • Activity: Day 12: Drive to Narvik via ferry Svolvær-Skutvik. Day 13: Explore Narvik - cable car, war museum, fjord cruise in Ofotfjord.

  • Highlight: Narvikfjellet cable car to 656m for panoramic views. War Museum detailing WWII Arctic battles. Midnight sun over fjords.

  • Overnight: Narvik Camping (2 nights). Driving distance: 250km + ferry.

Day 14:

Narvik to Tromsø & Departure

  • Activity: Morning drive to Tromsø (250km, 4 hours). Return motorhome at airport. Optional final activities: Arctic Cathedral, Polaria, cable car. Evening flight departure.

  • Highlight: Arctic Cathedral (Ishavskatedralen) - iconic architecture. Fjellheisen cable car for final Arctic views. Mack Brewery - world's northernmost.

  • Journey Summary: 14 days, ~2200km, Bergen to Tromsø, crossing Arctic Circle, experiencing fjords, mountains, and midnight sun.

Alpine Adventure:
Austrian Mountain Odyssey

Discover the majestic Austrian Alps on this breathtaking route through snow-capped peaks, crystal-clear lakes, and charming mountain villages. Experience the best of Austrian culture and natural beauty.

  • 10-12 days
  • ~1200 - 1500 km
‹
›
Day 1-2:

Arrival in Vienna & Imperial Capital

  • Activity: Day 1: Arrive Vienna Airport, motorhome pick-up. Day 2: Full day exploring Vienna - Schönbrunn Palace (UNESCO), St. Stephen's Cathedral, Hofburg Palace. Evening Vienna State Opera optional.

  • Highlight: Schönbrunn Palace gardens and Gloriette viewpoint. Historic center ring road (Ringstraße). Traditional coffee house experience.

  • Overnight: Wien Camping Neue Donau (2 nights). Distance: City exploration only.

Day 3:

Vienna to Wachau Valley & Melk

  • Activity: Drive along Danube to Wachau Valley (UNESCO). Visit Melk Abbey - Baroque masterpiece. Optional Danube cruise from Melk to Krems. Explore Dürnstein village.

  • Highlight: Melk Abbey library with 100,000 volumes. Dürnstein ruins with Danube views. Wachau apricot products and local wines.

  • Overnight: Campingplatz Melk or nearby. Driving distance: 90km.

Day 4:

Wachau to Salzburg

  • Activity: Drive to Salzburg (250km, 3 hours). Afternoon exploring Mozart's birthplace. Evening Sound of Music tour optional.

  • Highlight: Hohensalzburg Fortress (cable car or walk up). Mozart's Birthplace museum. Mirabell Palace gardens (Sound of Music filming location).

  • Overnight: Camping Nord-Sam (Salzburg) or nearby. Driving distance: 250km.

Day 5:

Salzburg to Hallstatt & Salzkammergut

  • Activity: Morning drive to Hallstatt (UNESCO). Boat tour on Hallstätter See. Visit Salt Mine (world's oldest). Explore Gosau lakes area.

  • Highlight: Hallstatt viewpoint (classic photo spot). Dachstein Ice Caves (5 Fingers viewing platform). Hallstatt Skywalk.

  • Overnight: Camping Klausner-Höll or Hallstatt camping. Driving distance: 80km.

Day 6:

Grossglockner High Alpine Road

  • Activity: Full day on Grossglockner High Alpine Road (48km toll road). Multiple stops at viewpoints, visitor centers, and hiking trails. Drive to highest point (Edelweißspitze 2571m).

  • Highlight: Kaiser-Franz-Josefs-Höhe viewpoint (Grossglockner 3798m). Pasterze Glacier - Austria's largest. Alpine wildlife observation (marmots, ibex).

  • Overnight: Heiligenblut camping or Matrei in Osttirol. Driving distance: 120km (including alpine road).

Day 7:

Heiligenblut to Zell am See

  • Activity: Morning in Heiligenblut - pilgrimage church visit. Drive to Zell am See (65km). Afternoon lake activities: boat cruise, swimming, or cycling around lake.

  • Highlight: Schmittenhöhe cable car to 2000m for panoramic views. Zell am See promenade. Boat tour on Lake Zell with mountain reflections.

  • Overnight: Camping Norden or Zell am See camping. Driving distance: 65km.

Day 8:

Zell am See to Kitzbühel

  • Activity: Short drive to Kitzbühel (45km). Explore historic town center. Optional hike or cable car to Kitzbüheler Horn (1996m) or Hahnenkamm (famous ski race).

  • Highlight: Kitzbühel old town with painted buildings. Hahnenkamm Streif downhill course (summer luge available). Schwarzsee lake.

  • Overnight: Camping Kitzbühel or nearby. Driving distance: 45km.

Day 9:

Kitzbühel to Innsbruck

  • Activity: Drive to Innsbruck (95km). Explore Tyrolean capital. Visit Golden Roof, Hofburg Palace, and Cathedral. Evening Nordkette cable car for sunset views.

  • Highlight: Nordkette cable car to Hafelekar (2256m) - "Top of Innsbruck". Golden Roof (Goldenes Dachl) - symbol of city. Bergisel Ski Jump viewing platform.

  • Overnight: Camping Innsbruck or Kranebitten camping. Driving distance: 95km.

Day 10:

Innsbruck to Stubai Glacier

  • Activity: Day trip to Stubai Glacier (40km). Glacier cable car to 3210m. Visit Ice Grotto, suspension bridge. Optional summer skiing/snowboarding.

  • Highlight: Stubai Glacier viewing platform at 3210m. TOP OF TYROL suspension bridge. Year-round snow activities. Panoramic restaurant with glacier views.

  • Overnight: Return to Innsbruck camping (same as previous night). Driving distance: 80km round trip.

Day 11:

Tyrolean Alps to Zillertal Valley

  • Activity: Drive through Tyrolean Alps to Zillertal Valley. Optional stops: Swarovski Crystal Worlds in Wattens, medieval Rattenberg (smallest town in Austria).

  • Highlight: Zillertal Alpine Road scenic drive. Mayrhofen cable cars to Ahorn (2000m) or Penken (2095m). Traditional Tyrolean dinner in mountain hut.

  • Overnight: Camping Mayrhofen or Zillertal camping. Driving distance: 100km.

Day 12:

Zillertal to Vienna & Departure

  • Activity: Morning drive back to Vienna (480km, 5-6 hours). Return motorhome at airport. Optional final stop at St. Florian Monastery (Baroque architecture) or Linz.

  • Highlight: Final alpine views through Tyrol. St. Florian Monastery (largest monastery in Upper Austria). Last Austrian coffee and pastry before departure.

  • Journey Summary: 12 days, ~1300km, Vienna circuit through Alps, 5 UNESCO sites, highest roads and glaciers, cultural and natural highlights.
